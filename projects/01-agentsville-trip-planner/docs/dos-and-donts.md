# Do And Don't

## Do

- Keep trip constraints explicit: dates, budget, traveler type, location, and preferences.
- Separate planning from validation.
- Preserve a small number of representative outputs when safe.

## Don't

- Do not assume generated travel facts are current without verification.
- Do not commit notebook outputs that contain credentials, private paths, or raw API responses with sensitive content.
- Do not claim the itinerary is valid unless supporting checks are documented.
